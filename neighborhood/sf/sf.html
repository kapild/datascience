San Francisco(S.F.) similarity using Menu data

There are about 32 neighborhoods in San Francisco(S.F.)

How to use this page.

    1. Neighborhood Map:
    1. Hover on the neighborhood map of your choice. This will give you the score of its top 10 neighborhood in the similarity bar.

    2. It will change the Menu item cloud for this neighborhood.



    2. Bigraph:
    1. You can click the neighborhood either on the left side of the bi-graph or right side. Clicking in the left will show its top 10 neighborhood, menu item similarity graph.

    2. By clicking on any of the top 10 next, will give you the common menu item cloud. These represent the top 50 common menu items in both the neighborhood.

    3. Start by selecting on right. By doing this you can see in what all neighborhoods this neighborhood is in its top list. Sort of reverse mapping.
